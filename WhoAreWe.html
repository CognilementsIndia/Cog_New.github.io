Champions for meaningful progress

We are a Customer Experience Design and Consultancy firm, specialising in research, design and building of digital first solutions to empower enterprises with a digital customer experience that just works.

Cognilements was founded by Samudragupta Talukdar in the year 2018 as a Business Consultancy Firm to drive “growth” and “vision” of various businesses. The idea of Cognilements was to bring the best of the corporate world to the MSME (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises), SME (Small and Medium Enterprises) and Corporate entities to drive their numbers. This would also include various strategies to take care of a major component called “Total Cost of Opportunity”.

We started our first project with Maharashtra's largest Travel and Logistics company "Prasanna Purple". Being a start-up consulting company our thought process was to join hands towards the growth and thus designed a solutions for the drivers which help them to boost their mileage of their vehicles.

To our learning we didnt ended up learning about the mileage but also the importance of "Customer Experience" as a service to boost your business. The solution not only helped them the objective of increasing profit margins but also allowed them to invest more on the Customer Experience part which was more service oriented.

Our values

Truth, Transparency and Communication

At Cognilements we value ourself in maintaining a culture to build a team around absolute elements of conducting business.

Truth:
At startups this is the toughest call to build an ecosystem, however in long run this is the most benefitting approach to build the team. Today we believe in accepting the truths and building confidence and supporting the teams, individuals and departments.

Transparency:
A start up is bound to make mistakes and people should not be afraid of mistakes and building an opaque and translucent story. We have been transparent in sharing our thought process and feedbacks.

Communication:
The vital component of any delivery or running an organisation is communication. At Cognilements we make sure that everyone is given equal opportunities to speak up their mind and put their thoughts across to anybody in the organization.
